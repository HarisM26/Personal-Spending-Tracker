Weekly report:

Category Report:
 - Total spending for Category
 - Percentage of limit used (for visualisation)
 - Available for savings/investments
 - Expenses sorted by size
 - Comparison of budget, limit used and total spending compared to previous week

 Overall Report:
 - Total spending
 - Percentage of total budget used
 - Total savings
 - Number of categories where limit was achieved
 - Comparison of all of the above to previous week
 - List of categories with percentage of budget used compared to previous week.
